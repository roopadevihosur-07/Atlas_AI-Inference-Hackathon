How it works

A

Angles

Each angle targets a distinct psychological lever — scarcity, social proof, aspiration, or problem/agitation. Testing multiple angles reveals which message resonates most with buyers.

M

Markets

Each market is a geographic + cultural target. Copy is written in the local language with culturally specific references — what works in New York fails in Tokyo without adaptation.

×

The Matrix

Every cell = one angle × one market = one production-ready ad. A 2×2 grid gives 4 variants to A/B test simultaneously. Brands running 50+ variants per week outperform those running 5.
